Catch-up after COVID: Margo brings 1760 Bush back to CCSD

In March 2023, Margo was appointed to a CCSD Committee to help bring the other Affiliated Tenant Associations out of dormancy after the COVID Era by helping Tenants and Boards re-engage. Margo was to help other TAs re-establish democratic operations, have regular meetings, represent tenants, and build community. The Committee was also tasked with helping Tenants understand how a TA operates by providing Officer Training so Tenants understood and could make informed decisions.

Over the summer, Margo had shared with the rest of the Board that she along with CCSD had started their work. She said CCSD and her were working on developing the Board Officer Training to start around August or September. She suggested we encourage all Tenants to attend those sessions so Tenants could understand what the Board is, what it does, and bring new people in for the roles.

Margo also suggested 1760 have a nomination and election in October and November for the Board. We all agreed that was a good plan and agreed to hold Nominations in October of 2024.
